Tesla

The Tesla car is an electronic self driving car ,Tesla has falcon-wing doors.Tesla was originally named after Nikola Tesla, the inventor of alternating current.The Tesla Roadster 2020 holds the record for the fastest electric car in the world. The founders of Tesla Motors were Eric Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning.Elon Musk joined the company later and then became CEO. Elon Musk loves easter eggs so much he included the ability to watch your car drive down rainbow road like in Mario Kart on its display panel.


SpaceX

SpaceX was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk. It is a private space company which builds rockets for space travel and satellites .SpaceX first rocket was called the Falcon 1. Elon Musk spent a third of his reported fortune, $100 million, to get SpaceX going.


Starlink

Starlink is thousands of satellites which have been launched into lower orbit around earth to enable Elon Musk to created a network so people everywhere can access the internet. Most recently he has used this method to help Ukranians during the war.
